Tony's Coffee Guide

My Personal Odyssey Through Dark Water

June 25, 2017 Tony Towry

2 minute read

  • Twitter

  • Email

I drink quite a bit of coffee. It’s true.

Occasionally it comes up in conversation. It occurs that someone is bored with their beans and wants to class up their caffeine delivery. Maybe this is you.

Well, here are some of my favorites as of late. Check them out.

Top Picks

  • The best espresso that may ever enter your face comes out of San Jose, CA. Gamut from Chromatic won’t let you down with this consistently delicious espresso. http://www.chromaticcoffee.com/gamut-espresso/
  • Novel is doing wonderful things. They keep a tight rotation, so there’s always something new and nothing specific to name, but I haven’t been let down. https://www.novelcoffeeroasters.com/
  • Onyx Coffee Lab out of Bentonville, AR is absolutely killing it. They’ve got an amazing space and do pretty much everything really well. Worth a stop if you’re in the area. Worth a try if you just want to order online. Plus, they offer a new level of transparency in http://sprudge.com/point-sale-tranparency-reporting-onyx-coffees-new-website-118712.html. https://onyxcoffeelab.com/
  • 1000 Faces is a little hit and miss. They’ve brought forth a formidable Guji and if you can catch them when they’re running Suke Quto it’s worth an order. The espressos, William & Maria, and Misty Valley I think of as an unremarkable, ok. http://www.1000facescoffee.com/
  • Populace puts out some really nice Ethiopians. I’ve had their Guji and a Peruvian, I may end up checking out their cold brew blend at some point. They’re reasonably priced and don’t have much to worry about on the shipping end. https://populace.coffee/

I’ll continue to keep my notes here, but I don’t plan on spamming the RSS with updates. Check back as needed.
