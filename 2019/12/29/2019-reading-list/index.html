This Year In Books

My 2019 Reading List

Published Sun, Dec 29, 2019 by Tony Towry

This year I read .916 books per month, which is several more books than I thought I’d be allowed to read given I have two small children.

Fiction

  • House Made of Dawn by N. Scott Momaday - This is a wonderful book and opens with writing reminiscent of East of Eden.
  • Brighton Rock by Graham Greene
  • Under the Volcano by Malcolm Lowry

Stats, Risk, Etc.

  • Trading Bases by Joe Peta
  • The Flaw of Averages by Sam Savage
  • The Tyranny of Metrics by Jerry Z. Muller - This is a fun counter read for someone just getting into FAIR or statistics.

Finance

  • The Geometry of Wealth: How To Shape A Life Of Money And Meaning by Brian Portnoy
  • The Laws of Wealth: Psychology and the secret to investing success by Daniel Crosby

Management / Leadership

  • The Coaching Habit: Say Less, Ask More & Change the Way You Lead Forever by Michael Bungay Stanier

Parenting

  • Dad is Fat by Jim Gaffigan I received this one for Christmas and finished it quickly. The best stories are up front, but don’t miss Losing My Religion and Snow Job.
  • Someone Could Get Hurt: A Memoir of Twenty-First-Century Parenthood by Drew Magary - I don’t recommend this one.
